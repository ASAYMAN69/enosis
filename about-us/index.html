LOGO THEORY

The concept for our logo is derived from the shape of human hands. As its name implies, 'enosis' means Unity or Friendship. The shape represents two hands coming together in unity, holding and touching each other to create a shelter. It also symbolizes the dreams kept inside that shelter. We naturally care for and create things that are valuable to us. When the rain comes and attempts to cause harm, we protect it with our palms.

Our logo explores the care we have and the emotion we put into our creations. Moreover, it is a symbol that inspires us in every moment to move forward and fulfill our commitments to client satisfaction by ensuring their safety, and providing superior products and services.
